STRENGTH & SHRED

KEY RULES
Follow these for maximum results
Schedule: Mon = Upper A · Tue = Lower A · Thu = Upper B · Fri = Lower B. Rest Wed/Sat/Sun.
Progressive Overload = #1 driver. Add weight or 1–2 extra reps when you hit the top of the range with good form.
Rep ranges: 4–6 = strength · 8–12 = hypertrophy · 12–15+ = endurance/burn
Rest: 2–3 min on big compounds, 60–90 sec on isolation.
Workout length: 45–60 min.
Warm-up: 5–10 min light cardio + 1–2 light warm-up sets of your first exercise.
Nutrition = 80% of fat loss: 300–500 cal deficit, 1.6–2.2 g protein/kg bodyweight.
Optional cardio: 20–30 min steady-state on rest days. Don't overdo it.
WEEKLY PROGRESSION
Weeks 1–2: Learn form, pick controllable weights.
Every 1–2 weeks: Hit top rep range → add 5–10 lbs next session.
After 4–6 weeks: Progress to advanced version or add drop sets.
